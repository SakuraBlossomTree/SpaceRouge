It is the year 2100.

You don't know where you are right now 

As you drift in space without any thought to who, where or why are you here in the first place.

"Hey you!!"

You heard a voice speaking to you.

You open your eyes to see a man in a suit in front of you.

You scan your surroundings. You are in a hospital, you see a heartrate monitor

It shows your pulse, and most importantly your heartrate 

50 bpm is the number you see, quite low for a person like you.

"Hey are you alright." The old man in the suit asks, you don't know him.

"You ship got destroyed and someone heard your distress signal and bought you here."

The man continues "After a few days you can get well enough then after that talk to me and I will offer you a deal."
