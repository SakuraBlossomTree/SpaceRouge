It is the year 2100.

Humanity transformed the world beyond recognition.

The cities are larger than ever. The networks span planets. 

Machines perform work once thought impossible.

Yet for all this progress, something feels lost.

More people replace their bodies with machinery every year. 

Entire orbital colonies are operated by artificial intelligences. 

Corporations hold more power than governments.

Flesh is weak.

Flesh is obsolete.

At least, that is what they keep telling us.

Beyond Earth, among the stations, colonies, and forgotten sectors of space, another future may still exist.
